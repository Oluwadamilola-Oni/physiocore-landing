Features

Everything your clinic needs,
in one place

Stop juggling paper files, WhatsApp messages, and spreadsheets. PhysioCore brings every part of your clinic under one roof.

📋

Patient Records

Register patients, document full clinical histories, track encounters, and generate discharge summaries — all stored securely in the cloud with automatic MRN assignment.

🤖

AI Differential Diagnosis

GPT-4o analyses your assessment findings and returns a ranked list of differential diagnoses with supporting clinical reasoning — at the point of care, in seconds.

🎙️

AI Medical Scribe

Hit record, see the patient, and documentation writes itself. The scribe captures the consultation and generates a complete SOAP note automatically. Saves 30–60 minutes daily.

🎥

Teleconsultation

Conduct secure video consultations directly from PhysioCore. Patients receive a join link by SMS — no app download required. AI generates the clinical note automatically when the call ends.

📱

Appointments & SMS Reminders

Book single or recurring appointments. Automatic SMS reminders at 72h, 24h, and 3h before each session. Teleconsultation links sent automatically in the confirmation SMS.

💳

Payments & Billing

Record cash, POS, bank transfer, and HMO payments. Generate itemised receipts instantly. Track outstanding balances, partial payments, and complete revenue history.

📦

Inventory Management NEW

Track and sell physiotherapy products — resistance bands, therabands, braces, and more. Manage suppliers, monitor stock levels, receive low-stock alerts, and sell directly through the payment page.

🏥

HMO Management

Configure your HMO providers and claim amounts per service. Track approvals, pending claims, and denials. Integrated directly into the billing workflow.

👥

Team Management

Five roles — Admin, Supervisor, Physiotherapist, Intern/Student, and Clinical Support Associate — each with granular permissions. Student notes require supervisor co-sign.

📊

Reports & Analytics

Revenue by payment method, HMO breakdown, daily trends, attendance, caseload analytics, outcome tracking, and condition-level reporting — all in real time.

🔒

Full Audit Trail

Every clinical and administrative action is logged with timestamp, user identity, and role. Tamper-evident, medicolegally defensible records your clinic can rely on.

📄

Clinical Documents

Generate professional discharge letters, upload X-rays and lab results, export full patient records as PDF — all with your clinic's letterhead and branding.

Security & Compliance

Patient data security
is not optional

PhysioCore is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with healthcare-grade security controls at every layer.

End-to-end encryption

All data encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS 1.3. Patient records never stored or transmitted in plain text.

Role-based access control

Five distinct roles with granular permissions. Staff only see what their role permits. Admins control all access from one panel.

Full audit trail

Every clinical and administrative action logged with timestamp, user identity, and role. Tamper-evident and medicolegally defensible.

Clinic data isolation

Multi-tenant architecture with Row-Level Security. No clinic can ever access another clinic's data — enforced at the database level.

Session security

Automatic logout after 15 minutes of inactivity. Force password change on first login. Secure credential management throughout.

NDPR & NDPA compliant

Built in full compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.
