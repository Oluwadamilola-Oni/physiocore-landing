Patient Records

Register patients, document full clinical histories, track encounters, and generate discharge summaries — all stored securely in the cloud.

AI Differential Diagnosis

GPT-4o analyses your assessment findings and suggests ranked differential diagnoses to support clinical reasoning at the point of care.

Book single or recurring appointments. Automatic SMS reminders sent to patients at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 3 hours before their appointment.

Security & Compliance

Patient data security is not optional

PhysioCore is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure with healthcare-grade security controls.

End-to-end encryption

All data encrypted at rest and in transit using TLS 1.3. Patient records are never stored or transmitted in plain text.

Role-based access

Five distinct roles with granular permissions. Staff only see what they need. Admins control all access from one panel.

Full audit trail

Every clinical and administrative action logged with timestamp, user identity, and role. Tamper-evident and medicolegally defensible.

Clinic data isolation

Multi-tenant architecture with Row-Level Security. No clinic can ever access another clinic's data — enforced at the database level.

Session security

Automatic session timeout after 15 minutes of inactivity. Force password change on first login. Secure credential management.

NDPR compliant

Built in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019 and the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023.
